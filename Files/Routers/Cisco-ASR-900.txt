### **Cisco-ASR-900 Series Router Specification File**

---

#### **General Information**
- **Model Name:** Cisco-ASR-900  
- **Product Line:** ASR 900 Series Aggregation Services Routers  
- **Manufacturer:** Cisco Systems, Inc.  
- **Release Date:** March 2024  
- **Form Factor:** 1RU Rack-Mountable  

---

#### **Technical Specifications**

##### **Hardware**
- **Processor:** Dual-core ARM Cortex-A72 @ 1.8 GHz  
- **Memory:**  
  - RAM: 16 GB DDR4  
  - Storage: 256 GB SSD (Expandable to 1 TB)  
- **Power Supply Options:**  
  - AC Power Supply: 100–240 V, 50–60 Hz  
  - DC Power Supply: -48 V  

##### **Interfaces**
- **WAN Ports:**  
  - 2 x 10G SFP+  
  - 1 x 40G QSFP  
- **LAN Ports:**  
  - 8 x 1G/10G SFP+  
  - 2 x RJ45 (1G Base-T)  
- **Management Ports:**  
  - 1 x RJ45 Console Port  
  - 1 x USB 3.0  

##### **Transmission Specifications**
- **Maximum Transmission Power (EIRP):**  
  - 23 dBm @ 2.4 GHz  
  - 26 dBm @ 5 GHz  
- **Carrier Frequencies:**  
  - 2.4 GHz Band: 2350–2450 MHz  
  - 5 GHz Band: 5150–5850 MHz  

##### **Wireless Standards Supported**  
- **Wi-Fi Protocols:**  
  - IEEE 802.11a/b/g/n/ac/ax  
- **Modulation Techniques:**  
  - OFDMA, QPSK, 16-QAM, 64-QAM, 256-QAM  
- **Channel Bandwidths Supported:**  
  - 20 MHz, 40 MHz, 80 MHz, 160 MHz  

---

#### **Routing Capabilities**
- **Routing Protocols:**  
  - OSPF, BGP, EIGRP, RIP, IS-IS  
  - MPLS Support: LDP, RSVP-TE  
- **IPv4/IPv6 Dual Stack:** Supported  
- **Maximum Routes:**  
  - IPv4: 1 Million  
  - IPv6: 750,000  

---

#### **Security Features**
- **Firewall:** Built-in Stateful Inspection Firewall  
- **Encryption:**  
  - IPSec VPN with AES-256/SHA-256  
  - TLS 1.3 Support  
- **Authentication Protocols:**  
  - 802.1X  
  - TACACS+ and RADIUS Integration  
- **Intrusion Detection/Prevention System (IDS/IPS):** Supported  

---

#### **Performance**
- **Throughput:**  
  - WAN-to-LAN: 40 Gbps  
  - Encrypted: 10 Gbps  
- **Concurrent Connections:** Up to 1 Million  
- **Latency:** < 1 ms for packet forwarding  
- **Jitter:** < 0.5 ms  

---

#### **Environmental Specifications**
- **Operating Temperature:** -20°C to 65°C  
- **Humidity:** 5%–95% non-condensing  
- **Cooling:** Dual-redundant hot-swappable fans  

---

#### **Software Specifications**
- **Operating System:** Cisco IOS XR  
- **Management Features:**  
  - Web-based GUI  
  - Command Line Interface (CLI)  
  - SNMP v3 Support  
  - REST API for Automation  

---

#### **Wireless Network Optimization**
- **Dynamic Channel Allocation:** Automatic based on interference levels  
- **Beamforming Technology:** Supported  
- **Transmit Power Control:** Adaptive, based on device proximity and interference  

---

#### **QoS (Quality of Service)**
- **Traffic Shaping and Prioritization:**  
  - Weighted Fair Queuing (WFQ)  
  - Low-Latency Queuing (LLQ)  
- **VoIP Optimization:** Built-in Call Admission Control (CAC)  

---

#### **Diagnostics and Monitoring**
- **Real-time Monitoring Tools:**  
  - CPU and Memory Utilization  
  - Interface Status and Traffic Analysis  
- **Logs:**  
  - Event Logging  
  - Syslog Support

  - Height: 44.45 mm (1.75 in)

#### **Supported Use Cases**
